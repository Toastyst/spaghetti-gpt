**AI News Digest: May 12, 2026**  
*Big Tech Advances & Coding Agents Heat Up*

### Top Developments from the Past 24 Hours

**Anthropic Launches Claude Platform on AWS & Agent View**  
Anthropic made the Claude Platform generally available on AWS, offering native API access with IAM authentication and unified billing. They also rolled out Agent View in Claude Code—a unified dashboard for managing multiple parallel coding sessions from the CLI. This boosts enterprise agentic workflows.
